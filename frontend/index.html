How Delivering Works

1
Choose a fuel type
Use the fuel buttons below to pick the exact fuel you want to deliver to the station.
2
Check the delivery details
Once selected, you’ll see the current station stock, payout rate, and your estimated return.
3
Enter your quantity
Type how many units you want to deliver. The panel will update your estimated payout automatically.
4
Deliver and get paid
Press Deliver to restock the station. If the payout pool can cover it, the reward is paid to your wallet.
